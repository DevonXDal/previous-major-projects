Detailed Description

A MeldSet is more of a virtual game support object than a real-world object. It represents the three or four cards that a player lays down at the end of a round that form a three-of-a-kind or four-of-a-kind such that there are three or four cards with the same rank but differing suits. Its primary purpose is to assist the scoring at the end of a round.
